Please be sure your email address and other contact information is correct by visiting our website at www.rushcard.com or calling us at 1-866-787-4227.

You agree that we do not need to provide you with a written (non-electronic) copy of any Statement, unless you specifically request us to send you a copy.

Your access to this page verifies that your system/device has the necessary software to permit you to receive and access PDF files.
